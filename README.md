### enigmatography
Noun (_uncountable_)
1. The art of making or solving puzzles.

Origin: Ancient Greek αἰνίγματος (ainigmatos) (genitive of αἴνιγμα (ainigma, “cryptic saying, riddle”)) +‎ -graphy.
